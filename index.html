About the Team

Green Gears is a team of driven engineering students participating from the United Arab Emirates University, striving to make an impact in the sustainable transportation industry. The team combines multiple skills and disciplines to produce innovative solutions. Green Gears have set their sights further than the regional competition and aim to make it to the global finals.

About Shell-Eco Marathon


The Shell Eco-marathon is a global competition that challenges student teams to design, build, and drive the most energy-efficient vehicles possible. Participants aim to travel the farthest distance using the least amount of energy.
